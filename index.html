Privacy Policy

Blood seekers cannot contact donors directly. Our Admin Panel will handle all communication to ensure donor privacy.

When blood is needed, our official Admin Panel will contact the donor to coordinate the process.

Donor privacy and security are our top priorities. No personal contact numbers are shared without consent.
